# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People keeping a personal shortlist of restaurants, bars, cafes, and food spots to try, revisit, compare, and remember. The app is used mostly in mobile or on-the-go contexts, with some planning and discovery time before deciding where to eat next.

## Product Purpose

Bite Marks helps users discover food spots, save them as personal spots, organize them with tags, and return to them later with useful context intact. Success means a user can quickly search nearby or known places, add promising spots, and build a lightweight food memory that stays practical over time.

## Brand Personality

Urban, minimalist, and lightly textured. The product should feel direct, location-aware, and a little tactile without becoming decorative or noisy. The voice should be plainspoken and useful, with enough personality to feel like a personal city notebook rather than generic database software.

## Anti-references

Do not make it feel like a marketing landing page, lifestyle blog, generic SaaS dashboard, or glossy restaurant reservation platform. Avoid oversized hero sections, cream-and-purple startup styling, heavy cards everywhere, cute food illustrations as the main identity, and decorative texture that gets in the way of scanning.

## Design Principles

- Put the workflow first: search, save, tag, and revisit should be obvious without explanation.
- Keep the surface compact and scannable, especially for mobile use outdoors or between decisions.
- Use texture as material detail, not ornament: subtle grain, ink, map, paper, or street-level cues can add identity when they support readability.
- Let discovery feel active: nearby context, tags, and saved spots should make it easy to notice a next place.
- Prefer confident restraint over polish for its own sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard accessibility for search, dialogs, navigation, and saved-spot actions. Support reduced-motion preferences and avoid relying on color alone for status or action meaning.
